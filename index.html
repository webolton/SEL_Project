Introduction

Welcome to Digital South English Legendary: Phase 1.

The Digital South English Legendary (DSEL) is a web-based, interactive application that will allow users to view multiple versions of individual texts from the South English Legendary at the same time. The internet is an ideal platform for publishing medieval texts with complicated textual traditions, because it can reveal the nuances that critical apparatuses often conceal and challenge the tendency of printed editions to privilege a “good” version of a text.

As of this stage of the project’s development, I have designed the basic navigation and appearance of the site, transcribed and marked-up a sample set of texts—the Life of Saint Botulf, and programed the first version of the application for viewing the texts. The project is ongoing and will be updated as different aspects of the site are completed.

The Digital South English Legendary was conceived, edited, and developed by William E. Bolton. Please feel free to email me at wbolton@asu[dot]com or find my contact information via my CV at my personal website.
